platform benefits

Transparency

Being hosted on smart contracts on the Ethereum Blockchain, users can look through our contract source code as well as track any transaction made on the contract to ensure BetKoin stays fair and relies on accurate game outcomes and does not act maliciously.

Be the house

Bets placed by users will always have the house to hedge against their bets - users can buy into the house funds and receive a split of the profits at the end of every quarter. Moreover, shares in the house will be tradeable at any time with other users at any convenient price for liquidity.

White Label

BetKoin will open it's API and custom houses on the platform for 3rd party providers to join in the platform with custom lines and APIs. This would ultimately benefit users of the platform with initial house buyouts.
