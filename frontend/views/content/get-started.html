How to get Started?

Transparency

Being hosted on smart contracts on the Ethereum Blockchain, users can look through our contract source code as well as track any transaction made on the contract to ensure BetKoin stays fair and relies on accurate game outcomes and does not act maliciously.

Highest security technology

Use of blockchain technology and constant improvements. Blockchain is designed to resist data changes. The information in the blockchain cannot be changed.

Accessibility

Since BetKoin is almost non-existent by the exchange rates, interest rates, transactions charges or other charges of any country; therefore it can be used at an international level without experiencing any problems. This, in turn, saves lots of time as well as money which is otherwise spent in transferring money from one country to the other. Betkoin operates at the universal level and hence makes transactions quite easy.

Highest speed with lowest cost

Instant, on-demand settlement. Low operational and liquidity costs of all the nodes in the system.
